This is Everest, also known as Kitty, Titten, and Lil Baby. My boyfriend and I rescued her from the Michigan Animal Rescue in Pontiac and it was the best decision we have ever made! We got her just a few days before my birthday and she was with us for Christmas! She is almost 1 year old. She terrorizes my dog, Bailey. Everest loves:

  • Knocking things off the counter
  • Sleeping in the sun
  • Running from room to room quickly for no reason
  • Swatting at running sink water
  • Annoying Bailey
  • Stalking in the grass on her kitty leash
  • Eating
  • Catching flies and the occasional mouse
